Chemical Engineering Department

The Chemical Engineering Department at NIT Srinagar is dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation in the field of chemical engineering. Our department offers a comprehensive curriculum that combines theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing students for successful careers in academia, industry, and research.

We provide state-of-the-art laboratories and facilities to support hands-on learning and cutting-edge research. Our faculty members are experienced professionals and researchers who are committed to mentoring students and fostering a collaborative learning environment.

Programs Offered:

  • B.Tech in Chemical Engineering: A four-year undergraduate program that covers fundamental concepts in chemical engineering, including process design, thermodynamics, reaction engineering, and transport phenomena.
  • M.Tech in Chemical Engineering: A two-year postgraduate program that delves deeper into advanced topics, research methodologies, and practical applications in chemical engineering.
  • Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering: A research-oriented program aimed at fostering innovation and contributing to the advancement of chemical engineering knowledge.
