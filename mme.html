Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering

The Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at our institute is dedicated to advancing the field of materials science and engineering. We offer comprehensive programs that cover the fundamentals of metallurgy, materials processing, and the development of new materials for various applications.

Our curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong theoretical foundation as well as practical skills through laboratory work, research projects, and industry collaborations. Graduates from our department are well-equipped to pursue careers in academia, research, and the materials industry.

Programs Offered:

  • B.Tech in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering: A four-year undergraduate program focusing on the principles of metallurgy, materials characterization, and processing techniques.
  • M.Tech in Materials Science and Engineering: A two-year postgraduate program that del ves deeper into advanced materials topics, research methodologies, and practical applications.
  • Ph.D. in Materials Science and Engineering: A research -oriented program aimed at fostering innovation and contributing to the advancement of materials science knowledge.
