Mechanical Engineering Department

The Mechanical Engineering Department at NIT Srinagar is dedicated to providing high-quality education and research opportunities in the field of mechanical engineering. Our department is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories and facilities to support both theoretical and practical learning.

We offer undergraduate (B.Tech), postgraduate (M.Tech), and doctoral (Ph.D.) programs designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the rapidly evolving field of mechanical engineering.

Programs Offered:

  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering: A four-year undergraduate program focusing on core mechanical engineering concepts, design, manufacturing, and thermal sciences.
  • M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering: A two-year postgraduate program that delves deeper into advanced mechanical engineering topics, research methodologies, and practical applications.
  • Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering: A research-oriented program aimed at fostering innovation and contributing to the advancement of mechanical engineering knowledge.
