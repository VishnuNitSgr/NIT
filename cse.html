About the Department:

The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Department at NIT Srinagar is dedicated to providing high-quality education and research opportunities in the field of computer science and engineering. Established in [Year], the department has grown to become one of the leading centers for computer science education in the region.

We offer undergraduate (B.Tech), postgraduate (M.Tech), and doctoral (Ph.D.) programs designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the rapidly evolving field of computer science and engineering.

Programs Offered:

  • B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering: A four-year undergraduate program focusing on core computer science concepts, programming, algorithms, data structures, and software engineering.
  • M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering: A two-year postgraduate program that delves deeper into advanced computer science topics, research methodologies, and practical applications.
  • Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering: A research-oriented program aimed at fostering innovation and contributing to the advancement of computer science knowledge.
