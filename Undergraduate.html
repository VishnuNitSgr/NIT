Undergraduate Programs

The undergraduate programs at our institute provide students with a strong foundation in engineering and technology. Through rigorous coursework, practical labs, and industry-oriented projects, students develop the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in their chosen fields.

Our programs emphasize innovation, critical thinking, and hands-on learning, preparing graduates to meet the demands of the modern engineering and technology sectors.

Available Programs: B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Metallurgical & Materials Engineering.
